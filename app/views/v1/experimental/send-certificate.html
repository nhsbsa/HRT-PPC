Do you wish to be emailed your HRT prepayment certificate details?

Important: Reminder:

This is an HRT PPC which only covers certain drugs. If you require drugs outside of HRT then a standard PPC may be more beneficial for you.

Information:

We do not send out plastic cards any more because they take time to reach you. They are also costly to the NHS and the environment.

The most convenient way to receive your HRT certificate details is by email. You will also get an email 1 month before the HRT PPC's expiry date, which can help you avoid any future costs or fines.

If you choose to receive your details by email you will get a:

  • certificate number
  • start date
  • end date
  • an email reminder 1 month before your renewal date

We'll send you your new certificate by email, and a convenient reminder 1 month before your renewal date.

You will also be able to print your certificate at the end of the service.

If you choose this option, you will receive your certificate by post and a postal reminder 1 month before your HRT PPC renewal date.

This can often be delayed, therefore we recommend choosing the email service.

You can print your certificate at the end of the service.
