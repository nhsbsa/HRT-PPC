Buy an NHS Hormone Replacement Therapy Prescription Prepayment Certificate (HRT PPC)

Important: Important information

Not all Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT) medicine is covered by an HRT Prescription Prepayment Certificate (HRT PPC). It's important to check your HRT medicine is covered before purchasing this certificate.

If your HRT medicine is not covered, or you need a prescription for something other than HRT, a different Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC) may be better for you.

About your HRT PPC

If you need more than 2 prescriptions within 12 months, an HRT PPC will save you money.

Each item on an NHS prescription usually costs £9.35. You can buy an HRT PPC for a one-off debit or credit card payment of £18.70 (the cost of two prescriptions).

This covers you for an unlimited number of HRT prescriptions for 12 months.

Information:

After buying an HRT PPC, you can claim a refund for any HRT prescriptions paid for in the last 30 days, but you must have a refund form for each prescription.

We prevent fraud by matching certificate details on our database with prescriptions. If your details do not match you may be fined.

If you are applying on behalf of someone else, fill in the application using their details rather than your own.

You cannot get a certificate by post through this online service. If you want a postal certificate there are other ways to buy one.
