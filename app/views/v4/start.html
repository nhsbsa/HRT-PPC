Buy an NHS Hormone Replacement Therapy Prescription Prepayment Certificate (HRT PPC)

Important: Important information

This Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC) only covers certain Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT) medicines.

If you need a prescription for something other than HRT, a PPC may be better for you.

You cannot get a certificate by post through this online service. If you want a postal certificate there are other ways to buy one.

You can save money on NHS HRT prescriptions by prepaying for them.

Each item on a NHS prescription usually costs £9.35. You can buy an HRT PPC for a one-off debit or credit card payment of £18.70 (the cost of two prescriptions)

This covers you for an unlimited number of HRT medications for 12 months.

Information:

Once you have bought an HRT PPC you cannot claim a refund for this.

You may be able to claim a refund for medicines already purchased that your certificate now covers.

Find out more about prescription refunds

Before you start

  • Check if your medicine is covered by the HRT PPC
  • We'll ask for your name, date of birth and postcode. These should be same as on your prescription to avoid any potential fines.
  • You may only use a debit or credit card

If you are applying on behalf of someone else, fill in the application using their details rather than your own.
