Buy an NHS Hormone Replacement Therapy Prescription Prepayment Certificate (HRT PPC)

Important: Important information:

This PPC only covers licensed Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT) treatments.

If you require prescription medication outside of HRT then a Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC) may be more beneficial for you.

Check here to find out if your HRT drugs are covered by the HRT PPC

Save money on NHS HRT prescriptions by prepaying for them.

A prescription costs £9.35 per item, but prepaying for an unlimited number of HRT prescriptions is:

  • one time payment of £18.70 for 12 months (the cost of two prescriptions)

Important information

You are not able to claim a refund for your HRT PPC.

Ways to receive your certificate

  • email
  • print a copy
  • save a PDF copy to your device
  • take a screenshot of the certificate

You are unable to receive your certificate by post through this online service but if you require a postal certificate there are other ways to buy one.

How to use your HRT PPC

Show your certificate details to your pharmacist when you collect your HRT prescription.

Your HRT PPC will start on the day you buy it – so if you buy it today it will be valid from today's date. This means you can collect your HRT prescriptions straight away. TBC

You can use your HRT PPC in any pharmacy within the UK.
