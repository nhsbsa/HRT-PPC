When do you want your HRT PPC to start?

You can choose to start your HRT PPC between 20 October 2023 and 20 December 2023.

If you collected an HRT prescription today, enter today's date. If not, enter the next date you will collect an HRT prescription. If this is over a month away, you should wait and buy your HRT PPC later.

For example, 15 9 2023
