Getting your HRT prescriptions

  1. Ask your GP to list medicines covered by the HRT PPC on a separate prescription to any other items you get. Your pharmacy needs this to dispense your medication.
  2. Before collecting your medicine, check that it is on the list of medicines covered by the HRT PPC.
  3. When collecting your HRT prescription, show your HRT PPC and tick box 'W' for HRT PPCs on the back of your prescription.

If you do not have your certificate details, the pharmacy will check the box 'Evidence not seen' on the back of your prescription. You'll not be charged.

Not all prescription forms have a tick box for HRT PPCs. If that is the case tick box 'F' for Prescription Prepayment Certificate.
