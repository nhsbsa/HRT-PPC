Buy an NHS Hormone Replacement Therapy Prescription Prepayment Certificate (HRT PPC)

Important: Important information

This PPC only covers certain Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT) medication.

If you require prescription medication for something other than HRT, a Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC) may be better for you.

Find out if your HRT medication is covered by the HRT PPC before you continue

You can save money on NHS HRT prescriptions by prepaying for them.

Each item on a NHS prescription usually costs {{ data.NHSPrescriptionCost }}. You can buy an HRT PPC for {{ data.HRTPPCCost }} (the cost of two prescriptions).

This covers you for an unlimited number of HRT medications for a full 12 months.

Ways to get your certificate

You can get your certificate by:

  • email
  • printing a copy
  • saving a PDF copy to your device
  • taking a screenshot of the certificate

You cannot get a certificate by post through this online service. If you require a postal certificate there are other ways to buy one here.

Important information

Once you have bought an HRT PPC you cannot claim a refund.
