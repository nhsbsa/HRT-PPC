You may save more with a PPC that covers all NHS prescriptions

Depending on how many of your prescriptions are for items not covered by an HRT PPC, you may save more buying a 3 or 12 month PPC that covers all NHS prescriptions. This includes all HRT medication as well as any other items you get prescribed.

HRT PPCs only cover certain HRT medicines.

A PPC will save you more than an HRT PPC if you get:

  • 4 or more items in a 3-month period and at least 2 are for items not covered by an HRT PPC
  • 12 or more items in a 12-month period and at least 10 are for items not covered by an HRT PPC

Find out more about PPCs that cover all NHS prescriptions on our website (opens in new tab).
