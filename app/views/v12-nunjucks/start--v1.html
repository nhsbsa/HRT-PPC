Use this service to buy an HRT PPC.

If you pay for NHS prescribed HRT medicine 3 or more times in 12 months, an HRT PPC could save you money.

Each item on an NHS prescription usually costs {{ data.NHSPrescriptionCost }}. You can buy an HRT PPC for a one-off payment of {{ data.HRTPPCCost }} (the cost of two items).

This covers you for an unlimited number of certain HRT prescriptions for 12 months.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • the name of your HRT medicine to check if it's covered – you can find this on your prescription or the medicine packaging
  • a valid credit or debit card to pay for the HRT PPC

If you already have an HRT PPC, check when it expires. You can buy a new one if there is less than one month remaining on your existing certificate.
