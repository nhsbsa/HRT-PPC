Some of your HRT medicines are not covered by the HRT PPC

An HRT PPC could still save you money if you need to pay for an HRT medicine that is covered 3 or more times in a 12-month period.

But you may save more with a PPC that covers all NHS prescriptions. This includes all HRT medication as well as any other items you get prescribed.

Deciding whether to buy a PPC

A PPC will save you more than an HRT PPC if you get either:

  • 4 or more items in a 3-month period and at least 2 are not for items covered by an HRT PPC
  • 12 or more items in a 12-month period and at least 10 are not for items covered by an HRT PPC
