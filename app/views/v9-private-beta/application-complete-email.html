How to use your certificate

When collecting your HRT prescription, tick box 'F' on the back of your prescription. TBC

Not all prescription forms have a tick box for HRT PPCs. If that's the case you should tick box 'F' for Prescription prepayment certificate.

You will need to show your HRT PPC. If you do not have your certificate details, the pharmacy will check the box 'Evidence not seen' on the back of your prescription. You will not be charged.
