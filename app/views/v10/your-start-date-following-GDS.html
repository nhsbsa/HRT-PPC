When do you want your HRT PPC to start?

You can choose to start your HRT PPC between 20 October 2023 and 20 December 2023.

If you collect your next prescription after 20 December 2023, you can apply later to get the most out of your HRT PPC. You can start your HRT PPC up to one calendar month after the date you apply.

Choose the date you will collect your next prescription to get the most out of your HRT PPC.

If you already have a valid exemption check when it expires. Depending on the type of certificate you have you may be able to use our Check if you have an NHS exemption service.

For example, 15 9 2023

If you have paid for HRT medicine in the last month, you may be able to get a refund of the prescription charge. You can only claim a refund if you got an FP57 receipt when you paid for them.
