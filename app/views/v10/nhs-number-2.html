What is your NHS number?

Your NHS number can help us match your details correctly.

If you do not know your NHS number, use our online find your NHS number service.

Your NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.
Other ways to find your NHS number

If you're registered with a GP, you will have a 10 digit NHS number unique to you.

It is usually on any letter or document you have received from the NHS, such as:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • appointment letters
  • maternity notes
  • the NHS app
