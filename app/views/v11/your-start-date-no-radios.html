When do you want your HRT PPC to start?

You can choose any day to start your prepayment certificate between 20 October 2022 and 20 December 2022.

For example, 10 12 2022
When should I start my HRT PPC?

If you have not paid for your HRT medicine yet, choose the date you will collect your next prescription to get the most out of your HRT PPC.

If you have paid for HRT medicine in the last month, you may be able to get a refund of the prescription charge if you backdate your HRT PPC.

You can only get a refund if:

  • you got an NHS refund form (FP57) when you paid for your NHS prescription - you cannot get one later
  • you backdate your HRT PPC to cover the date you paid for your NHS prescription
  • the HRT PPC covers your HRT medicine

Do not backdate your HRT PPC if you cannot get a refund of the prescription charge. Instead, choose the date you will collect your next prescription to get the most out of your HRT PPC.
