Medicine which will be covered by the HRT PPC

Not all medicine will be covered by the HRT PPC. It's important to check that the medicine you require will be eligible before purchasing this certificate.

If the medicine you need is not listed here, check with your GP or pharmacist before purchasing a PPC.

As other medicine becomes licensed for the treatment of HRT, it may be added to this list.
